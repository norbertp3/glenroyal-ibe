THE REDWORTH HALL HOTEL

Redworth Hall is a stunning 17th century Jacobean Manor House situated in the blissful tranquility of its own 26 acres of landscaped grounds. Part of The Cairn Collection, our signature four-star hotel is home to 143 bedrooms, The Retreat fitness and beauty rooms as well as a number of function rooms hosting up to 250 guests, making it the perfect destination for business or pleasure.
